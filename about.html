About DEK360

Discover the story behind Ghana's premier content platform.

Telling Ghana's Story to the World

DEK360 Ghana is a leading digital media platform dedicated to showcasing the vibrant culture, rich heritage, and dynamic contemporary scene of Ghana. Founded with a passion for authentic storytelling, we bring our audience the best of Ghanaian entertainment, news, and cultural insights.

From in-depth cultural features to trending news coverage, from street food adventures to music reviews, DEK360 covers it all. Our mission is simple: to be the bridge between Ghana and the world, sharing stories that inform, entertain, and inspire.

With over 173,000 subscribers and 2,900+ videos on YouTube, DEK360 has established itself as one of the most trusted voices in Ghanaian digital media. Our community spans the globe, connecting diasporans with their roots and introducing international audiences to the beauty of Ghana.
